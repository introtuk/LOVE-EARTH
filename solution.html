AFFORESTATION

Afforestation is the establishment of a forest or stand of trees in an area where there was no previous tree cover.

Facts :-
1.A tree can sequester up to 150 kilograms of carbon dioxide per year.
2.Using wood for construction saves about 40 percent of carbon emissions in comparison to concrete, and about 30 percent in comparison to steel.
