GLOBAL WARMING

Global warming is the long-term warming of the planet's overall temperature.

CAUSES:-
1. Burning fossil fuels.
2. Deforestation & Tree-Clearing,etc.

FACTS:-
1. More than 1 million species are at risk of extinction by climate change
2.Average sea level is expected to rise between 0.5 and 1.5 metres before the end of the century.

DEFORESTATION

Deforestation is the purposeful clearing of forested land.

CAUSES:-
1. Expansion ,Infrastructure and Mining.
2. Industrial Agriculture.

FACTS:-
1.We Lose Around 10 Million Hectares of Forest Every Single Year.
2.Deforestation Contributes about 4.8 Billion Tonnes of Carbon Dioxide A Year.

PLASTIC

Plastic pollution is the accumulation of plastic objects and particles in the Earth's environment that adversely affects humans, wildlife and their habitat.

FACTS:-
1. Only 9% of all plastic produced is recycled. 2. A whopping 2 million plastic bags are used every minute worldwide.
3. Every minute of every day a truckload of plastic is dumped into the the ocean.

5. The average person eats 70,000 microplastics each year.
